AWS Cost & Posture Review.

Who this is for.

This is the right engagement if your AWS bill runs $50,000 a month or more, you suspect 10-25% of it is waste, your security team has flagged AWS posture issues nobody got around to following up on, and you want a written, dollar-quantified diagnostic from a single senior operator instead of a four-week engagement from a consultancy that sends three junior people.

What you get.

A written report.
Ranked, dollar-quantified findings across 10 domains: cost waste, security posture, orphaned resources, DR readiness, lifecycle, CloudFormation drift, tag governance, observability, service quotas, network topology.
A 90-day roadmap.
Sequenced by ROI and effort, with suggested ownership.
A 90-minute walkthrough.
With up to 5 of your team members. Live video. I answer questions about every finding.

How it works.

Day 1–2
You provision read-only IAM credentials using the published policy at /policy/. I run Kulshan, my audit tool.
Day 3–4
I validate findings against your Cost Explorer and Cost & Usage Reports.
Day 5
60-minute interview with whoever owns the AWS bill or architecture.
Day 6–9
I write the report and roadmap.
Day 10
Delivery. PDF report, 90-day roadmap, walkthrough call scheduled within 5 business days.

Total time investment from your side: about three hours.

Pricing.

Fixed fee, scoped on a 20-minute call. Most reviews land in a narrow range for a standard AWS environment. I tell you the exact number before you commit to anything. If a full ten-domain review is more than you need, I also do single-pack reviews (cost-only or security-only) in one week. Same depth, narrower scope.

About me.

I'm Yuvdeep Singh. I spent five years at AWS as a Senior Solutions Architect and TAM. I built Kulshan because every consulting engagement started with the same five hours of manual checks; I got tired of repeating it.
