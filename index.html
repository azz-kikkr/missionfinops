2. Authenticate

$ kulshan report

Why I built Kulshan

Over six years working with AWS customers. Hundreds of AWS environments. The same pattern kept showing up.

Teams discuss solutions before understanding the baseline.

They buy platforms before understanding the problem. They open Jira storms before knowing which accounts, services, or regions actually matter.

Kulshan began as the audit I kept performing manually. Cost Explorer, then cross-referencing, then documenting findings, then summarizing for the customer. Over time it became a CLI.

Run one command. Get one report. Start from facts.

Then decide whether you need Athena, Cost Intelligence Dashboards, CloudZero, Vantage, consultants, or nothing at all.
