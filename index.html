Why I started Mission FinOps

Most teams chase savings before they understand the bill.

I was a network engineer before cloud. The first question was always simple:

"What changed?"

After six years helping AWS customers, I saw the same pattern over and over.

Teams rarely struggled to find recommendations. They struggled to agree on what was actually happening.

Kulshan became the free baseline.

Mission FinOps became the human conversation that follows.
