Why I started Mission FinOps

Most teams chase savings before they understand the bill.

I was a network engineer before cloud. That shaped how I look at AWS cost: follow the system, find the owner, and explain the tradeoff in plain English.

After six years inside AWS, I saw the same pattern across customers. The hard part was rarely finding a discount. It was getting finance, engineering, and leadership to agree on what was actually happening.

I built Kulshan as the free baseline, then started Mission FinOps for the human part: helping teams understand the evidence, decide what matters, and follow through.
